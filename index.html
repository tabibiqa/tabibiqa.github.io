About Us

Tabibi is a medical institution that aims at integrating the best of modern, traditional and complementary medicine in an ideal world. It is offered by a well-functioning group of physicians/ therapists centered to the patients. Tabibi balances curative services with preventive care by monitoring healthcare trends from research and clinical observation.

The fundamental basis of running this establishment is to provide optimum care for people and improve the incidence and prevalence of physical and mental illnesses. With the increasing consumer expectation for healthcare, it is adapted to include advice on management of lifestyle related chronic diseases and sustaining health in the aging population by providing essential health services in time.

Our Mission

Providing a distinguished and pioneering treatment in modern medicine and complementary medicine in an integrated way and using the highest quality standards under the supervision of experts in modern medicine and complementary medicine for the benefit of our customers and society.

Our Vision

We aspire to be an integrated national and regional research and treatment center for modern medicine and complementary medicine.

Our Value

Please our valued customers through Providing distinguished therapeutic, awareness and research services in the field of complementary medicine that benefit society and spread awareness and fill the shortage in the academic and research side.
